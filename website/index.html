Why AshAI?

It's like having a whole team — but you just talk to one person.

Multi-agent teams

Ash orchestrates specialized agents — researchers, writers, coders — that work together on your task. You just say what you need.

Just talk to it

No menus to navigate, no modes to learn. Open AshAI, type what you want, and Ash handles the rest. It's a conversation.

Your data, your control

Everything runs locally or in your own cloud. Your conversations and data never touch our servers. Bring your own API keys.

Any AI provider

Works with Anthropic Claude, OpenAI GPT, Google Gemini, or local models via Ollama. Switch freely — no lock-in.

Extensible plugins

Add new tools and capabilities with a simple plugin system. Give your agents access to databases, APIs, or custom workflows.

Open source

MIT licensed. Read the code, contribute, self-host — it's yours. Built in public by developers who use it daily.

How it works

1

Open AshAI and say what you need

Use the web app or desktop app. No account needed for the desktop version. Just type or paste your request — plain English is all you need.

2

Ash assembles the right team

Ash reads your request and spins up specialized agents — a researcher, a coder, an analyst — whatever the task needs. You see everything happening in real time.

3

Agents work together, report back to you

Your agents coordinate behind the scenes. They share context, divide up work, and deliver results back through Ash. Ask follow-ups, give feedback, iterate — just keep talking.
